Company Overview

Lyceum Engineering & Construction Pvt. Limited (LECPL) is a leading multidisciplinary Engineering, Procurement and Construction Management (EPCM) contracting company,

delivering integrated solutions across Process, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation, Control Systems, Fire & Gas, Civil, Quality, Plant Integrity, Asset Management and HSE disciplines. With a proven record of success in Engineering, Design, Construction, and Commissioning, LECPL brings technical excellence, disciplined project execution, and a commitment to safety and quality in every engagement. LECPL’s in-house capabilities cover the full project life cycle, from Feasibility Studies and TechnoCommercial Evaluations to Conceptual, Basic, and Detailed Engineering, as well as Project Management and Project Management Consultancy. We specialize in delivering turnkey EPC/EPCC solutions for both greenfield and brownfield developments, including design optimization, capacity enhancement, unit addition and integration, and system de-bottlenecking.

Growth and Strategic Development

Following a strategic restructuring in 2010, Lyceum Technologies underwent a pivotal shift, prioritizing business development and cultivating international partnerships. This phase marked a deliberate expansion of the company’s operational scope and market reach. Through persistent effort and strategic business realignment, the company successfully navigated early challenges, solidifying its operational foundation.

The establishment of its sister company, Lyceum Engineering & Construction Pvt. Limited, in Pakistan in 2018 was a key strategic initiative, significantly enhancing localized project execution capabilities and regional presence. This development was further strengthened in 2025 through a restructuring of the Pakistan entity, which introduced an expanded leadership team and an enhanced governance framework. These steps have strategically positioned the organization for sustained growth and superior project delivery, aligning with its long-term strategic imperatives.

Our Vision

Lyceum Engineering & Construction Pvt. Limited (LECPL) aspires to be a recognized leader in Pakistan and Global engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning (EPCC) industry, setting new benchmarks for quality, safety, innovation, and client satisfaction. Our vision is firmly grounded in our mission, values, and corporate policies, driving us to deliver projects that contribute to national and international development, strengthen industrial capability, and uphold international standards of excellence.

MISSION STATEMENT:
  • To advance Pakistan’s human resource capabilities in the field of science, engineering, and technology, empowering professionals to meet global standards of excellence
  • To transform Pakistan’s natural and industrial resources into valuable assets through the application of state-of-the-art, efficient, and sustainable technology
  • To lead in delivering innovative, cost-effective solutions that address evolving technological challenges and enhance the performance of existing operations
  • To uphold the highest standards of safety, quality, and environment stewardship, ensuring the protection of the people, assets, and the planet

  • Service: We believe that serving others is the highest form of service – a reflection of integrity, humility and purpose in everything we do
  • People: We value our human capital above all, recongnizing our employees as the corner stone of progress, innovation and sustainability
  • Client Commitment: We honour our client’s trust by exceeding expectations and creating enduring partnerships built on mutual success
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and ethical conduct as the foundation of confidence, credibility and peace of mind
