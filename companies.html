Company Overview

Founded in 2006 in Alberta, Canada, Lyceum Technologies was established by professional engineers with a vision to deliver premier engineering services. Over time, the company strategically evolved, expanding its footprint to key global markets including North America, the Middle East, and the Eastern regions. Following strategic restructuring in 2014, leadership consolidated under Mr. Waseem Ahmad, P.Eng., who has since driven the company’s growth alongside a dedicated management team. In 2018, Lyceum Engineering & Construction Pvt. Limited was established in Pakistan as a sister company to support expanded project execution, with further organizational strengthening in 2025 through the addition of new directors and enhanced governance.

Growth and Strategic Development

Following a strategic restructuring in 2010, Lyceum Technologies underwent a pivotal shift, prioritizing business development and cultivating international partnerships. This phase marked a deliberate expansion of the company’s operational scope and market reach. Through persistent effort and strategic business realignment, the company successfully navigated early challenges, solidifying its operational foundation.

The establishment of its sister company, Lyceum Engineering & Construction Pvt. Limited, in Pakistan in 2018 was a key strategic initiative, significantly enhancing localized project execution capabilities and regional presence. This development was further strengthened in 2025 through a restructuring of the Pakistan entity, which introduced an expanded leadership team and an enhanced governance framework. These steps have strategically positioned the organization for sustained growth and superior project delivery, aligning with its long-term strategic imperatives.

Vision and Mission

Lyceum Technologies strives to be a global leader in Engineering, Procurement, and Construction Management (EPCM), engineering consultancy and turnkey project contracting, driven by our mission to develop human resources, harness cutting-edge technology, enhance asset safety, and protect the environment. Our vision is grounded in delivering exceptional value to clients by combining innovative solutions with operational excellence.

Services Portfolio
